Our Story

Eating here feels like
dinner at grandma's house.

For over three decades, Sofia has welcomed travellers and locals to her table on the port road of Pigadia. What began as a small family kitchen has become one of Karpathos' most-loved tavernas — not because of trends, but because of heart.

Every dish is built on island tradition: seafood landed the same morning, vegetables from nearby gardens, olive oil pressed on the island, and recipes passed down through generations. And of course — a warm, heartfelt welcome from Sofia herself.
